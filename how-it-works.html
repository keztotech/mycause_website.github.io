How MyCause Works?

MyCause is the best place to fundraise for what you care about, whether you are an individual, group, or organization.

1. Generate Request

On our site (android & iOS), users can create a Ration fundraising request to cover the grocery expenses required to feed their families.

2. Request Approval

After the request is submitted on MyCause, the request will be thoroughly reviewed. After the evaluation process, the request will be approved or rejected.

3. Receive Funds

The request will be displayed on the site (android & iOS), after it has been approved so that crowdfunding can be started resultantly

4. Collect Rations

Once the funds have been successfully completed, individuals can then visit any of our partner grocery stores to collect their rations. Furthermore, the collected funds will be transferred to the respective grocery store’s account.
