Two-Way Verification Process

MyCause Ensuring
Authenticity and Transparency

In a world where determining who deserves help and charity, reaching them, assessing their needs, and distinguishing between deserving individuals and potential fraud pose significant challenges, MyCause provides a solution.

With a rigorous two-way verification process, MyCause guarantees that every request on its platform is 100% authentic. This process ensures that donors can confidently contribute, knowing that their assistance reaches those truly in need. By implementing strict measures to prevent fake requests, MyCause upholds transparency and ensures that genuine help reaches deserving individuals, fostering trust and making a tangible difference in communities.

At MyCause, our platform is dedicated to assisting low-income families who are actively striving to improve their situation but face challenges amidst inflation. We do not engage with beggars; instead, we focus on empowering individuals who are working hard to make a difference in their lives. By providing support to these families, we aim to alleviate the difficulties they face in managing their essential needs. Through our platform, we foster a spirit of self-reliance and enable individuals to take control of their circumstances, fostering resilience and promoting positive change within their communities.
