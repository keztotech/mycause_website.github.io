Questions about fundraising

You start your GoFundMe by telling your story and setting a goal. You will then receive a fundraising page to accept donations and share your fundraiser. Signing up is easy and every donation is yours to keep, whether or not you reach your goal. GoFundMe makes it incredibly easy to raise money online for the things that matter to you most. In just a few minutes, you’ll be able to personalize your fundraiser and share it with the people in your life. In fact, over $9 billion has been raised by individuals on GoFundMe.
