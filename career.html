We are seeking an experienced Flutter Mobile Developer to join our dynamic team. As a Flutter Mobile Developer, you will be responsible for developing high-quality mobile applications using Flutter framework. The ideal candidate should have a minimum of 2 years of experience in Flutter development, along with a strong understanding of third-party APIs, websockets, and state management using Provider.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain high-quality mobile applications using Flutter framework.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Implement third-party APIs to enhance the functionality of mobile applications.
  • Utilize websockets for real-time communication and data synchronization.
  • Apply best practices in software development to ensure efficient and reliable code.
  • Implement and manage state management using Provider or similar frameworks.
  • Stay updated with the latest trends and advancements in Flutter and mobile development.
  • Debug and fix issues, and continuously improve the performance and user experience of the applications.

Requirements

  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in mobile app development using Flutter framework.
  • Proficiency in Dart programming language and strong understanding of Flutter architecture.
  • Experience working with third-party APIs and integrating them into mobile applications.
  • Familiarity with websockets and real-time communication protocols.
  • Strong knowledge of state management using Provider or similar frameworks.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills to work effectively in a team environment.
  • Solid understanding of mobile app development best practices, including performance optimization and code quality.
