Our Features

Salient Features of MyCause that make it the perfect platform

100% authentication

MyCause uses rigorous authentication methods to verify the cases and requests on the platform

Algorithm ends to end

MyCause uses an algorithm that will decide the requirement and target donations for a recipient based on their background.

Donation Transparency

All the records of the donations will be visible to everyone on the donation request. This will ensure transparency and accountability.

Audit Report

live audit report on the history of donations and funds will be available on the platform for everyone to see.

No money concept

The recipients of the donations will not receive any donations in the form of direct cash. They will be instead provided the services they seek.

User friendly

Donations on the platforms are totally free and no deductions will be applied to the amount that is donated.
